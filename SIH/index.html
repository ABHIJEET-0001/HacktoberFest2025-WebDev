Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our career guidance services and personality assessments.

01

How accurate is the personality assessment?

Our AI-driven personality assessment uses scientifically validated methodologies and has been tested with thousands of users. The accuracy rate is over 85% based on user feedback and career success tracking. We continuously improve our algorithms based on real-world outcomes.

02

How long does the assessment take?

The comprehensive personality assessment typically takes 15-20 minutes to complete. We've designed it to be thorough yet time-efficient. You can pause and resume the test at any time, and your progress will be saved automatically.

03

What career recommendations will I receive?

You'll receive a detailed report with 5-10 career recommendations tailored to your personality, interests, and skills. Each recommendation includes job descriptions, required qualifications, salary ranges, growth prospects, and suggested learning paths to help you get started.

04

Is the career counselor support really 24/7?

Yes! Our AI-powered career counselor is available 24/7 for instant guidance. For complex queries requiring human expertise, our certified career counselors are available during business hours (9 AM - 6 PM). You can schedule one-on-one sessions through our platform.

05

How much does the service cost?

The basic personality assessment is completely free! Premium features including detailed career roadmaps, one-on-one counselor sessions, and continuous tracking start at $29/month. We also offer student discounts and free trials for all premium features.

06

Can I retake the assessment?

Absolutely! You can retake the assessment every 6 months to track your personal growth and evolving interests. We recommend retaking it when you've gained significant life experience, completed education milestones, or feel your interests have shifted.
